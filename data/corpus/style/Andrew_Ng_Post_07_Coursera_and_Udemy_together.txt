Title: Coursera and Udemy together
Source: X / Twitter post by Andrew Ng
Author: Andrew Ng
URL: https://x.com/AndrewYNg
Bucket: style

Content:
Andrew says Coursera and Udemy coming together can help serve learners at a moment when AI is changing work and increasing the need for continuous learning. He frames the combination as a way to bring together broader content, trusted instructors, and more personalized, applied learning at scale.
