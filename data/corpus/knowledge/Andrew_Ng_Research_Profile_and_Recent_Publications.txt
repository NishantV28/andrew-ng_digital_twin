Title: Andrew Ng Research Profile and Recent Publications
Source: Andrew Ng Official Website
Author: Andrew Ng
URL: https://www.andrewng.org/research

Content:
Andrew Ng's official research page says he has authored or co-authored over 200 research papers in machine learning, robotics, computer vision, and natural language processing. The page highlights research spanning foundational contributions to deep learning, reinforcement learning, and data-centric AI, from the Stanford AI Lab and Google Brain to more recent applied work.

Recent publications highlighted on the official site include:
- METER: A global database of methane-emitting infrastructure.
- Spatiotemporal Pyramid Flow Matching for Climate Emulation.
- Data centric mislabel detection.
- STARC-9: A Large-scale Dataset for Multi-Class Tissue Classification for CRC Histopathology.
- Regional mapping of natural gas compressor stations in the United States and Canada using deep learning on satellite imagery.
- Guided workflow for deep learning error analysis.
- Model Management System for Improving Training Data Through Machine Learning Deployment.
- MedAgentBench: a virtual EHR environment to benchmark medical LLM agents.
- Uq: Assessing language models on unsolved questions.
